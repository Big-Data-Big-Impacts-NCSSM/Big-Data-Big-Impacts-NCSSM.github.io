Big Data Big Impacts is a group of students from the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics. who are dedicated to giving back to our community through software development. We create engaging websites and mobile apps for charity organizations, all for free. We are passionate and knowledgeable about computer science, programming, and software development, and we are committed to applying these skills for the improvement of our community.
